Meet the Teams

How a round works
  • 5 rounds — each team starts one round and picks first
  • Then the other 4 teams each pick one clue in that round
  • Correct = earn the points. Wrong = lose the points
  • Scores shown after every round
How to answer
Josh reads a clue as a statement. Your team responds in the form of a question:

"What is...?"  or  "Who is...?"
EXAMPLE
💬 "This is the document that shows whether a business made a profit or a loss over a period of time."
↓
✅ "What is a Profit & Loss statement?"
